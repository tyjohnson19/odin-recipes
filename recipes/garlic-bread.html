Garlic Bread

Here's how to make the BEST garlic bread! Two classic garlic bread methods: toasted and crisped under the broiler, the other soft from being wrapped in aluminum foil and heated in the oven.


Ingredients

  • 1 16-ounce loaf Italian or French bread
  • 1/2 Cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large cloves garlic, smashed and minced
  • 1 heaping tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cut the loaf in half, horizontally
  3. Mix the butter, garlic and parsley together in a small bowl
  4. Spread butter mixture over the two bread halves
  5. Heat in oven for 10 minutes on sturdy baking pan
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle parmesan cheese over bread
  7. Return to oven on highest rack for additional 2-3 minutes
  8. Remove from oven, let cool a minute. Remove from pan and use a bread knife to cut into 1-inch thick slices.
  9. Serve immediately.
